cho em

This is a song I wrote for a beautiful, talented, and radiant young woman. I want to express my gratitude for the immense inspiration she has given me.

This is a song I wrote to whisper to someone dear that I will wait, unwavering, with a fragile yet unyielding hope that one day, fate will lead them back to me.

This is a song I wrote with the heartfelt wish that they remain happy, their laughter never fades, and that somewhere, in the quiet corners of time, they are still waiting for me.

This is a song I wrote when I had just moved to a bustling city, where I had to learn to embrace a new rhythm of life, tangled with its own troubles. And then, in the midst of it all, I found myself longing for the loved ones who still waited for me in that distant place.

My Artistic Hobbies
Song Writing
Playing Musical Instrument

Through writing songs, I can express my stories and emotions in the most genuine way—no idealization, no romanticization, just the simple truths I wish to convey.

One of my wishes is to play as many musical instruments as possible, even though I have no natural musical talent—which is quite amusing. As of now, I can play the guitar, ukulele, cajon, a little bit of bass, and I’m currently learning the violin. I hope that one day, I will be able to fulfill this dream of mine.

Honestly, I’m not very good at drawing. I just want to capture the things that bring me peace. Maybe one day, I’ll take up sketching. Then, I’ll travel here and there, sketching the life around me.
